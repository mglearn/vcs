Digital Citizenship Reminder

StickyBoard is for respectful group thinking. Use it like a classroom wall, not a private chat.

  • Post ideas that are useful to the activity.
  • Do not include private information, student names, insults, or off-topic comments.
  • Use clear, professional language.
  • The facilitator may approve, edit, hide, or delete sticky notes.
