Digital Citizenship Reminder

WonderWall is for respectful, classroom-ready questions. Submissions are anonymous to the public, but the space is still part of our learning environment.

  • Ask questions that help the group learn.
  • Do not include names, private information, jokes at someone’s expense, or harmful language.
  • Use a professional tone. Staff and students should follow campus, district, and workshop expectations.
  • The moderator may edit, hide, or delete questions before they appear publicly.
WonderWall is a free, self-hosted alternative for moderated classroom and PD question walls.
