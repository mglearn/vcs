Digital Citizenship Reminder

StickyBoard is a learning space. Even when names are optional, every note should be appropriate for a classroom, workshop, or staff meeting.

  • Share ideas that help the group think, plan, solve, or reflect.
  • Do not include private information, student names, jokes at someone’s expense, or harmful language.
  • Use a professional tone and follow campus, district, and workshop expectations.
  • If moderation is on, notes appear only after approval. If moderation is off, the facilitator may still hide or delete notes.
StickyBoard is a free, self-hosted Jamboard-style sticky-note canvas for classrooms, workshops, and staff PD.
