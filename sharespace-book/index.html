Digital Citizenship Reminder

ShareSpace is a public learning gallery. Submit work that is respectful, appropriate, and safe to share.

  • Only upload your own work or work you have permission to share.
  • Do not include private information, student IDs, addresses, phone numbers, or sensitive records.
  • Use professional language and follow classroom, campus, district, or workshop expectations.
  • The facilitator may approve, edit, hide, or delete submissions.
ShareSpace is a free, self-hosted alternative for classroom and PD showcase boards.
